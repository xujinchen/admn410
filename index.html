Why use us

- Compare with other pet stores, we provide various product to our customers which include local and imported products. We have strict check process to ensure our products are harmless to pets. We own both online and physical stores. In addition, our physical stores are all located in downtown area. All of these makes our customers easier to make their choice than other stores.

History

- Our company was founded on May 5th,2007 by William Bruce. We only sold pet food at first. After several years development and market research, we found that pet toy also has a large market potential so we started it. So far we own 4 chain of stores and we intend to provide best pet products to our customers.
